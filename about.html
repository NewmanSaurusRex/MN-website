about M

Hey there! I'm M Newman, a Front-end Developer, Educator and Problem Solver based in Los Angeles.

I believe empathy is the best strategy when it comes to solving problems. I aspire to build products that increase access, equity and justice for folks.

Act I: As a human with ADHD, I was egregiously let down by traditional school, so I became a teacher to "make it right". I believed that if I could work within a detrimental system, that I could be an agent of change, offering support to students in the way that was so desperately missing from my own experience. I spent 11 years crafting a learner-centric, evidence-oriented classroom that maintained individual strengths while elevating challenges. My classroom was a collaborative, learning machine! But learning never once took precedence over building a trusting, kind and equitable environment.

Act II: Unfortunately, teaching during the Covid-19 pandemic highlighted layers of inequity that I could no longer ignore.
